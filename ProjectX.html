Project X

Explanation

Before starting the project, I did a bit of research to gather ideas and inspiration. I came across several different project concepts such as a restaurant menu, a website redesign, a fitness application, and more.

To be honest, I initially considered creating a fitness app since going to the gym is one of my hobbies and passions. However, after a recent travel experience, I came up with my own unique idea. I found it not only interesting but also a great challenge in terms of coding and development.

1. Project Plan

My project plan includes three main sections:

  • Subject – topic of the Project X
  • Context – outlining the background and inspiration behind the project
  • Problem Statement – identifying the issue the project aims to solve
  • Methodology and Delivery – explaining the process and how the final product will be presented
2. Current Progress

I have successfully coded the e-book and implemented interactive page-turning functionality. The e-book now includes a content page as well as individual pages for three different stories. Each element has been carefully structured to support a smooth and engaging reading experience. 3. Discovery

Throughout the development process, I gained valuable insights into how to structure and implement interactive features in an e-book. From creating a navigable content page to integrating multiple stories, each step has deepened my understanding of web development techniques for digital publications.
